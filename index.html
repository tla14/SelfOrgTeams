What is Collaboration

Collaboration is a working practice where individulas work together to a comon purpose to achieve business benefit. Collaboration enables individulas to work togetther to achieve a defined and common business purpose. It exists in two forms: Synchronous and Asynchronus.

Implemented in a business

Collaboration can be implemented into a business through trust. Trust among many colleagues is the only way to reduce the potential of conflict or rivalry, and enable collaborators to reach positive and feasible results.

Implemented in a business (cont.)

Networking:
Whether you are starting your business or just beginning to look for partners, the connections you make while networking offer great potential. When you meet someone who offers services that would mesh well with yours, pay attention! Think about the services your customers ask about most often.

Implemented in a business (cont.)

Another way to implement collaboration in a business is to listen to the voice of the employee. Employees should be included in the decision making process. Online collaboration platforms, in that regard, provide social business engagement with personal profiles, document sharing, groups, blogs, tags, social flow, RSS feeds, and polls.

Importance in a Business

Working in teams enables employees to be quicker and more effective in their work, as compared to people who work on projects on their own. Collaborating also makes employees more responsible, which goes a log way in raising their motivation levels, especially when teams work virtually.
